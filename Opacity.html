Opacity is the degree to which content behind an element is hidden, and is the opposity of transparency. Opacity applies to the element as a whole, including its contents, even though the value is not inhereted by child elements. Thus, the element and its chldren all have the same opacity relative to the element's background, even if they have different opacities relative to one another. If you do not want to apply opacity to child elements, us the background property instead.
